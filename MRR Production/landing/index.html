The story

Raised the old way. Built for a smarter one.

Steadwerk started with a kid raised in the old way. Amish roots taught that work is done right or done again, that you show up before the sun and don't complain. But he kept asking a question the old way couldn't answer: What if the tools worked as hard as we do?

Not to replace the work, but to carry the part of it that steals your evenings. So Steadwerk was born in Fort Wayne, Indiana: warehouse and fleet software with a work ethic, so the work runs steady, and the people who do it get home for supper.
